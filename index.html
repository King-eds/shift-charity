About Us

SHIFT is a non-profit organization committed to ending hunger by providing essential food supplies to those who are homeless and hungry. Our mission is to provide immediate relief to individuals in need while working to address the root causes of hunger in communities.

We collaborate with local organizations, food banks, and volunteers to ensure that no one goes hungry.
